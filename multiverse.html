Origin of the multiverse

The origin of the multiverse concept blends ancient philosophy, religious thought, and modern physics, proposing our universe is one of many; scientifically, it stems from cosmological inflation theory, suggesting rapid early expansion created 'bubble' universes, and quantum mechanics' Many-Worlds Interpretation, where every quantum outcome spawns a new reality, while historically, Greek Atomists and thinkers like Giordano Bruno pondered infinite worlds.
